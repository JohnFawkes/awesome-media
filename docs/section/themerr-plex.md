# THEMERR-PLEX

[Themerr-plex](https://github.com/LizardByte/Themerr-plex) is a Plex plugin that adds theme music to your movie and TV show libraries, sourced from sites like TheAudioDB and YouTube. Part of the [LizardByte](https://app.lizardbyte.dev/) family of Themerr plugins for Plex, Jellyfin, and Emby.
